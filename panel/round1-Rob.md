# Rob — Brand/visual designer (panel round1)

Freelance brand/visual designer. I run paced client walkthroughs + the odd portfolio panel,
maybe 2–3x/MONTH. Price-sensitive. My honest baseline is "I could just glance at my phone clock."

**(1) Purpose clear in 5s? — Yes.**
Headline "Showclock / Know exactly how far behind you are — and what time everything starts now"
plus the pre-filled agenda box and "Start show" told me instantly: it's a live run-of-show timer
that tracks drift. The subline "We track the drift and reflow every clock time live" sealed it.
No jargon, nothing confused me. A designer opens this cold and gets it.

**(2) Valuable in principle? — Yes. Would I personally reach for it? — Marginal/No.**
In principle this is genuinely good. I pasted "Intro 1m / Concept A 8m / Concept B 8m / Feedback 10m"
and it parsed every line including the 1-min item, showed a clean rundown with planned→projected
times, a projected end, and a Next button driven by Space. The drift pill ("1 min ahead" / "2 min
behind") is the real win — that's the thing a phone clock can NOT do: it reflows every downstream
start time live so I know whether to compress Concept B. THAT earns its place. But honestly, at my
2–3x/MONTH cadence, for a 4-item client walkthrough I can hold in my head, the phone glance usually
still wins for ME. The value is real; my frequency just doesn't justify the open. That's legitimate,
not a knock on the app.

**STAGE CUES (what I was asked to beat up):**
- Band sequence works fully: GREEN on a fresh 8-min item ("on time"), AMBER (the 1-min item is
  amber from the start — correct, the whole minute is inside the 60s floor), RED on overrun showing
  "+0:03 / over the planned 1 min". Color logic is sound and readable on my calibrated monitor.
- Sound toggle: DEFAULT OFF, found in <5s — top-right "🔔 Sound: Off", toggled cleanly to "On".
  Good that it's off by default; an auto-chime in a client room would be mortifying.
- Pulse: calm, NOT a strobe. Red card animates `cue-pulse` at 1.2s infinite — a slow breathing
  pulse. I would not be embarrassed projecting that. A sub-300ms flash would've killed it.
- Does color+chime beat a phone glance for me? For overrun awareness during a LONGER multi-segment
  session, the band + reflow genuinely beats a phone. For my short, infrequent walkthroughs:
  phone-glance still wins for me personally. Both true.

**(3) Advocacy: 6/10.**
It's well-built, legible, and the live reflow is the one feature a phone clock can't fake — that's
the hook I'd mention. I dock it because (a) it's a frequency mismatch for MY use, so I wouldn't bring
it up unprompted, and (b) nothing here made me go "I NEED this" the way a background remover does — a
run-of-show timer is a nice-to-have for an occasional MC, not daily grunt-work relief. I'd recommend
it to a friend who MCs weekly conferences without hesitation; for a fellow low-frequency designer I'd
shrug. A solid, honest 6 — not a polite 7.
