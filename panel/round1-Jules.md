# Jules — Content & community marketer (panel round1)

**Zero-login:** Yes. Landed straight on a paste-your-agenda box, no email/account wall. I'd have bailed in 5s if there were one — there isn't. Big plus.

## (1) Purpose clear in 5s?
**Partially.** I'd tell a friend: "Paste your run of show, it gives you a live timer + a per-item countdown so you know which segment you're on and how far ahead/behind you are." What helped: "Agenda — one item per line", the pre-filled greyed example, "Start show", and "See a live example". What confused me for a beat: the H1 promise "Know exactly how far behind you are — and what time everything starts now" reads like a *drift/scheduling* tool, not the "big timer for my phone podium" I came for. The timer IS the star once you Start, but the landing copy undersells it. I expected the word "timer" somewhere above the fold and didn't see it.

## STAGE CUES — exercised
- **Band color machine works.** Confirmed live in real time on a 1-min item: GREEN when comfortably ahead (saw green on the 5m & 20m items, "6 min ahead"), AMBER for the whole short item (a 1-min item sits inside the 60s floor so it opens amber — sensible), then RED at overrun showing "+0:08 / over the planned 1 min" counting up. Clean, legible.
- **Pulse: CALM, not strobe.** The red overrun band pulses opacity 1.0→0.72→1.0, 1.2s ease-in-out, no color flash, no hard blink. On a podium phone that reads as a gentle "you're over" breathe — I would NOT be embarrassed to have this glowing on stage. Good restraint.
- **🔔 Sound toggle: findable instantly.** Top-right, "🔔 Sound: Off" by default. Found it in ~2s with zero hint. Clicked → "🔔 Sound: On". Clear.
- **Phone-podium legibility (375px):** Excellent. Huge "Welcome / +0:08", full-width color band, fat Back/Next buttons, "Space = next" hint. Glanceable from a podium.
- **Co-host read-only link:** "Copy current link" works, copies a self-contained `#...` URL. Opening it in a fresh browser shows the same running view with banner "You're viewing a snapshot — ask the host to re-share, or refresh, to see the latest." IMPORTANT: it's a **snapshot**, not a live mirror — the co-host's clock ticks down from the shared moment but does NOT follow my Next clicks. The copy even says "Shares a snapshot of right now." That's honest, but it's not the "hand my co-host a live read-only link" I wanted; they'd drift from me within a segment or two.

## (2) Valuable to me (weekly AMA, phone podium, co-host link)?
**Marginal→Yes.** Today I wing it with my phone's clock app + a Notion doc of the run of show, eyeballing the gap. Showclock genuinely beats that: paste once, get the big countdown + per-item color + projected end times — that's real time saved every week and the no-login/no-setup is exactly my speed. The co-host "live link" is the one thing that falls short of my ask: it's a snapshot, so my co-host can't truly follow along hands-free.

Held back by two things: (a) landing copy frames it as a "how far behind" drift tracker, not the "podium timer" it actually is — I almost didn't realize the timer was the point; and (b) the co-host link is a *snapshot*, not a live shared view, which is exactly the feature I came for. Fix the co-host link to truly mirror the host (or rename it so I don't expect mirroring) and add the word "timer" up top, and this jumps to a 9 I'd bring up unprompted in my facilitator Discord.
